Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Allegro 2 Reformer come fully assembled?

No, the Allegro 2 Reformer comes partially assembled. Assembly includes attaching the footbar, springs, ropes, and risers. Most customers complete assembly in about 30 minutes.

Is the Allegro 2 suitable for home or studio use?

The Allegro 2 Reformer is designed to perform well in both home and studio settings thanks to a durable build and smooth, quiet operation.

Does the Allegro 2 Reformer come with springs?

Yes! The Allegro 2 Reformer includes 5 Balanced Body Signature Springs: 3 Red for medium resistance, 1 Blue for heavy resistance, and 1 Yellow for light resistance.

Can you increase the height of the Allegro 2 Reformer?

Yes, the Leg and Post Kit can be added at any time to convert the standard 8.5" height to a 15" studio height.

Can the Allegro 2 be stored vertically?

Yes, the Allegro 2 Reformer can be stored vertically with the footbar properly positioned and secured with the optional Wheel Kit and Wall Security Strap.

Does the Allegro 2 come with a warranty?

Yes! The Allegro 2 Reformer has a 10-year warranty from date of invoice.
